Whisked away by a bird, Theia finds herself far away from home. As day turns to night and strange creatures begin to wake, Theia must scavenge and survive as she journeys home.

OVERVIEW

Theia goes out on a routine gathering session.  When she’s up on the roof of the house gathering wood and such, she is suddenly snatched up by a bird and whisked away. 

She struggles and fights her way out of its grasp and falls onto a flower and upon seeing a white fence wall, she realizes she’s on the other side of the garden.  She finds herself lost and bumps into a firefly who she befriends and agrees to help her get home.  As the night progresses, many strange creatures come out and attack her.  With the guidance of the firefly, she fights her way past the creatures.


Morning approaches and Theia and the firefly get closer to her home.  A massive centipede appears and chases the two to Theia’s home. It wraps its long body around the house squeezing it, trying to draw them out. The firefly rushes outside trying to distract the centipede to lure it away, as it is distracted Theia grabs a piece of glass to reflect the rising sun’s raise at the beast. Causing it to burn…

PROSPECTIVE FEATURES

Theia is a Survival-horror Action RPG that infuses a similar combat system to that of Final Fantasy XV with the limited open world that comes with games like Dark Souls, Assassin’s Creed, and Skyrim.

  • Start off the game with a knife and already gathered materials to craft at the beginning of the game
  • Battle against bigger creatures will occur on occasion
  • The creatures will be less aggressive and appear infrequently at the beginning, but as the night goes on, more come out and become more aggressive
  • There will be an onscreen clock and at specific times of the night, drastic changes will occur amongst the creatures
  • Stealth and hiding are recommended after a certain point in the night as items and materials will be scarcer and harder to find, forcing the player to conserve any single-use items when they can
  • There are hiding places all over the area, creatures can’t get into things, but they can climb on top of them so careful when you jump up to places
  • The player can use the firefly for light, but be careful, light attracts creatures to your location
